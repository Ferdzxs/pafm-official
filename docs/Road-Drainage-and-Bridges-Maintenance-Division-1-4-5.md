# Road, Drainage, and Bridges Maintenance Division
## B. Declogging / Desilting, Crack Sealing, Installation of Manhole Cover, and Repair of Sidewalk / Curb and Gutter / Inlets

For the maintenance and repair of manhole cover, sidewalk/curb, and gutter/inlets in Quezon City.

## Service Information

- **Office/Division:** Road, Drainage, and Bridges Maintenance Division
- **Classification:** Highly Technical
- **Type of Transaction:** G2C (Government to Citizen)
- **Who may avail:** Residents of Quezon City

## Checklist of Requirements

### Requirement
Letter-request submitted through the official email address, Facebook page, or Department of Engineering Viber Community, with the following information:

- Name of requestor
- Exact location of reported area/site involved
- Contact number
- Photos

### Where to secure
- Applicant / Client

## Process Flow

### Client Step 1
Submit the letter-request or report through any of the following:

- Official email address: `engineering@quezoncity.gov.ph`
- Department Facebook page: `https://www.facebook.com/QuezonCityDepartmentofEngineering`
- Department Viber Community
- Walk-in

### Agency Action 1.1
Letter-request is received by the Records Section and routed to the Area Engineer or concerned agencies for appropriate action.

- **Fees to be paid:** None
- **Processing time:** 1 day
- **Persons responsible:**
  - Administrative Officer V (Records Officer III), Quezon City Department of Engineering
  - Engineer IV, Quezon City Department of Engineering
  - Senior Administrative Assistant II (Computer Operator IV), Quezon City Department of Engineering
  - Administrative Officer V

### Agency Action 1.2
Inspection by District Engineer and evaluation of the reported area/site involved.

#### Agency Action 1.2.1
Preparation of Program of Works.

- **Fees to be paid:** None
- **Processing time:** 7 days
- **Person responsible:**
  - Engineer IV, Quezon City Department of Engineering

### Agency Action 1.3
Inform the client of the inspection status.

#### Agency Action 1.3.1
Approval of project is subject to availability of materials.

- **Fees to be paid:** None
- **Processing time:** 15 days
- **Persons responsible:**
  - Engineer IV, Quezon City Department of Engineering
  - Administrative Officer V (Supply Officer III), Quezon City Department of Engineering

## Total
- **Fees:** None
- **Total processing time:** 23 days
