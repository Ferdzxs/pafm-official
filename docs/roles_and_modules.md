# Role and Module Responsibilities

## 1. Cemetery & Burial Management
*   **Cemetery Office** *(Manages burial applications, registries, graves, niches, and records)*
*   **SSDD** *(Coordinates indigent subsidies, citizen records, and burial aid)*
*   **Death Registration** *(Verifies death certificates and manages the processing of received burial documents)*
*   **Citizen** *(Submits burial applications, securely manages their documents via 'My Documents', and tracks their burial application status via 'My Applications')*

## 2. Facility Reservation System (Barangay)
*   **Reservation Officer** *(Reviews initial facility reservation records, permits, and payments)*
*   **Punong Barangay** *(Provides final approval for pending barangay requests and references ordinances)*
*   **Barangay Secretary** *(Handles constituent records, documents, filings, and manages barangay reports)*
*   **Citizen** *(Applies to reserve barangay facilities, manages required documents via 'My Documents', and tracks their reservation status via 'My Applications')*

## 3. Parks & Recreation Scheduling
*   **Parks Administrator** *(Oversees park venues, large-scale reservations, site usage logs, and manages the master booking calendar)*
*   **Citizen** *(Submits park reservation applications and tracks their booking status via 'My Applications')*

## 4. Water Supply & Drainage (Utility & Engineering)
*   **Utility Helpdesk** *(Triages incoming tickets for water connections, leaks, and drainage, and assigns them to field teams)*
*   **Utility Engineering** *(Executes physical fixes, tracks service tickets, leak reports, and completes installations for water supply and drainage)*
*   **Citizen** *(Requests water connections, reports leaks, submits drainage issues, and tracks the resolution status of those tickets via 'My Applications')*

## 5. Asset Inventory Tracker
*   **CGSD Management** *(Oversees overarching inventory, performs ocular inspections, and handles final approval records)*
*   **FAMCD** *(Directly logs inventory, requests inspections, and manages physical submission records for assets)*
*   **Cemetery Office** *(Acts as a Requesting Party to submit requests for cemetery supplies and maintenance)*
*   **Parks Administrator** *(Acts as a Requesting Party to submit requests for park supplies and maintenance)*
*   **Punong Barangay** *(Acts as a Requesting Party to submit requests for barangay hall supplies and maintenance)*

## 6. Finance & Treasury
*   **Treasurer Officer** *(Processes payment transactions, reconciles financial queues, generates revenue reports, issues official receipts, and reviews finance audits)*
*   **Citizen** *(Views their personal payment history, settles bills, and tracks official receipts for any paid services)*

## 7. System Administration
*   **System Administrator** *(Configures global settings, oversees user & role management, manages employee masters, views system audit logs, and controls legacy data migrations)*
